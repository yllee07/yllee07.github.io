I am interested in **geometric analysis**, **harmonic analysis**, and the areas where they meet **partial differential equations**, **geometry**, and **number theory**. I have been working on a series of expository projects aimed at making modern mathematical developments more accessible.

## Current Projects

- **50 Years of Geometric Analysis** — a systematic synthesis of seventeen talks from the 2026 Tsinghua conference, covering geometric flows, minimal surfaces, Kähler geometry, and PDEs. [PDF](/files/50_Years_Geometric_Analysis_Vol1.pdf)

- **Introduction to Modern Harmonic Analysis** — ongoing lecture-note style exposition on decoupling theory, Schrödinger operators, and Fourier analysis on manifolds.

## Notes and Writings

- *Fermat's Last Theorem: A Detailed Exposition* — a self-contained reconstruction of Wiles's proof, from algebraic number theory to modular forms.
- *The Weil Conjectures* — an introduction to the algebraic geometry underlying the Weil conjectures.
- *Notes on Modern Algebra* — from group theory through category theory, commutative algebra, and homological algebra.
- *Complete Solutions to IMO 2026 Problems* — full solutions to all six problems. [PDF](/files/2026_IMO_Solution__Y__Lee_.pdf)
- *Exercise Solutions to Evans' PDE* — complete solutions to all exercises in Evans's *Partial Differential Equations*.
